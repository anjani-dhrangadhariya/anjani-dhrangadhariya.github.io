I am a Research Assistant at <a href='http://medgift.hevs.ch/wordpress/'>MedGIFT group</a>, HES-SO, Valais-Wallis. I obtained my MSc. from [University of Bonn](https://www.uni-bonn.de/), [Bonn-Aachen International Center for Information Technology (B-IT)](http://www.b-it-center.de/) under the supervision of [Prof. Dr. Martin Hofmann-Apitius Lehmann](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Martin_Hofmann-Apitius). Prior to that, I did my BSc at [Christ College](https://www.christcollegerajkot.edu.in/), Rajkot, supervised by [Prof. Dr. John J Georrge](http://scholar.google.co.in/citations?user=q0Xkx-QAAAAJ&hl=en). My research interest are in the area of Natural Language Processing, Machine and Deep learning, Semantic web and ontologies. I am also interested in the area of text-aided image retrieval.
